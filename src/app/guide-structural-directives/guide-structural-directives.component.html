Structural Directives

One structural directive per host element

* is a syntactic sugar

The asterisk (*) prefix

*ngIf="exp as value" will be translated into:

ng-template [ngIf]="exp" let-value="ngIf" /ng-template

*ngFor="let item of [1,2,3] as items; trackBy: myTrack; index as i" will be translated into:

ng-template ngFor let-item [ngForOf]="[1,2,3]" let-items="ngForOf" [ngForTrackBy]="myTrack" let-i="index" /ng-template

*ngIf="hero" will be translated into - ng-template [ngIf]="hero"
